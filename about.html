About

The ACM Club at Kean University is more than just a student organization—it’s a thriving community of innovators, problem-solvers, and future tech leaders. Our club is dedicated to fostering a deeper understanding of computer science through hands-on experience, collaboration,and mentorship. We provide resources and opportunities that empower students to excel in areas like software development, hardware engineering, artificial intelligence, and more. With a focus on real-world applications, we offer access to internships, REU programs, and industry insights that prepare you for a successful career in the ever-evolving tech landscape. Whether you’re a seasoned coder or just starting out, the ACM Club is here to support your journey. Discover new technologies, expand your professional network, and develop the skills that will set you apart in the competitive tech industry. At ACM, we believe in helping every member reach their full potential through knowledge sharing, teamwork, and a passion for innovation.
